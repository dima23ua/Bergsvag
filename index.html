About company

The concept was inspired by the original architecture of the House itself: the geometric elements of the front facade, which attract attention, the bright and spacious rooms with five-meter ceilings, immediately send to the aesthetics of Scandinavian.

The creators of the project wanted to take you to the hygge atmosphere. Where everything is saturated with comfort, warmth and the smell of homemade cakes with cinnamon.

Penthouses

There are several penthouses on the seventh floor of the House. At the disposal of their residents: high ceilings of 5.5 meters, free planning to create a stylish, solemn interior.

Comfort zone

The House has its own fitness area, on the ground floor, for the services of residents and their guests: a cafe and a grocery store, a pharmacy, dry cleaning, a beauty salon and a medical center.

In the northern part of the house, for small residents, there is a well-equipped playground for entertainment. An open eco-terrace in the eastern part of the house, quiet and protected from city noise.
